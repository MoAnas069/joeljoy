Sell With Joel

Your property deserves
a premium strategy.

Selling a home requires more than a listing — it requires a proven strategy built on accurate pricing, intelligent marketing, and skillful negotiation. Joel's 6-step seller system ensures your property reaches the right buyers at the right price, every time.

01

CMA Report

Joel begins with a Comparative Market Analysis — a detailed report that examines recent comparable sales, active listings, and market trends in your specific neighborhood. This data-driven approach establishes the foundation for strategic pricing.

02

Home Evaluation

A comprehensive in-person evaluation of your property's unique features, condition, and presentation opportunities. Joel identifies exactly what makes your home stand out and recommends strategic improvements that maximize perceived value without overspending.

03

Marketing Plan

Joel develops a bespoke marketing campaign tailored to your property — professional photography, cinematic video tours, targeted digital advertising, and exposure across premium listing platforms. Your home is positioned as a luxury experience, not just a listing.

04

Price Negotiations

When offers arrive, Joel evaluates every term — not just price. Contingencies, timelines, financing strength, and seller protections are all analyzed. His expert negotiation ensures you receive the strongest possible deal with terms that work in your favor.

05

Inspections & Repairs

Joel manages the inspection process strategically — reviewing findings with you, advising on which repair requests are reasonable, and negotiating credits or fixes that protect your bottom line without derailing the transaction.

06

Contract to Closing

From accepted offer to closing day, Joel coordinates every detail — title work, appraisal management, document reviews, and timeline management. He keeps you informed at every stage so there are no surprises at the closing table.
